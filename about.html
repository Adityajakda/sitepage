About B R Thewa Art Jewellery

This thewa art is very popular in rajasthan and it is gold over glass work and in bottom silver work

Discover the allure of Rajasthan's heritage with B R Thewa Art Jewellery. We specialize in crafting stunning Thewa jewelry, blending 23-karat gold with vibrant glass in intricate designs that echo tradition and timeless elegance. Our artisans handcraft each piece, preserving cultural richness and skillful artistry in every necklace, earring, pendant, and bangle we offer. Explore the captivating world of Thewa craftsmanship at B R Thewa Art Jewellery, where each creation tells a story of heritage and beauty.

"Thewa Treasures: Crafted with Heart, Worn with Grace."
